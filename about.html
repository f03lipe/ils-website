About

The Ivy Leadership Council is a student-run organization of Ivy League student leaders dedicated to fostering future leadership domestically and globally. Each year, the Council hosts the Ivy Leadership Summit (ILS), a two-day conference that brings together students from the eight Ivy League universities along with leaders from the business, government, academic, and humanitarian sectors.

The conference will focus on how the youth can be empowered to turn an idea into a reality and create positive global change through leadership and compassion. This Summit seeks to inspire and educate students through motivating keynote speakers, breakout sessions, and student-led conversations about the steps we can each take to make those courageous steps in actualizing ideas into concrete action that will positively impact the world.
